Powerful Native Leaders

Sharice Davids (in office 2019-current)
She is a Ho-Chunk Native and a Democratic Representaive from Kansas. She was the first LGBTQ Native to be elected and one of the first 2 Native women ever elected into office.

Deb Haaland (in office 2019-current)
She is a Laguna Pueblo Native and a Democratic Representative from New Mexico. She is currently nominated for the position of US Secretary of the Interior under the Biden administration.
